Prince Zuko of the Fire Nation

“My name is Zuko, son of Ursa and Fire-Lord Ozai, prince of the Fire Nation and heir to the throne!”

Personality:

  • Brash & Hot Tempered
  • Honest to a fault

Early Life:

Zuko was a child born under the protective wing of his mother. Zuko loved his mother dearly this fact drove his younger sister, Azula to resent him for a time.

Zuko's relationship with his father was very strict and professional. Zuko never felt like he had any worth to his father in youth.

A mother's love:

Zuko, please, my love, listen to me. Everything I've done, I've done to protect you. Remember this, Zuko. No matter how things may seem to change, never forget who you are.
